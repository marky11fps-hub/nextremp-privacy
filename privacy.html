NextRep Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

NextRep ("the app," "we," "us") is a scheduling app for student-athletes. This policy explains what information the app handles and how. The short version: your data stays on your device, we don't track you, and we don't sell or share your information.

NextRep has no user accounts and no NextRep-operated database of your personal information. Your schedule, classes, practices, games, assignments, and academic details are stored locally on your own device.

Information stored on your device

When you use NextRep, the information you enter — including your class and practice times, games, assignments, sleep and meal preferences, GPA and study goals, and generated schedules — is stored locally on your device using your device's standard app storage. This information is not transmitted to us and is not accessible to us. If you delete the app, or use the app's "Delete all data" option, this information is removed from your device.

Connecting your school calendar (Canvas and similar)

NextRep lets you optionally connect your school's learning management system (such as Canvas) by adding your personal calendar feed link. When you do this, the feed link and the assignment and event information imported from it are stored only on your device. This information is never sent to our servers. NextRep reads your calendar feed directly from your device to display your assignments and due dates. You can disconnect at any time, which removes the feed link from your device, and you can choose whether to keep or remove any imported assignments.

The AI features

NextRep includes optional AI-assisted features, such as drafting a message to a professor and a schedule assistant that responds to typed requests. When you use one of these features, the text of your request is sent over an encrypted connection to our processing service, which forwards it to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate a response that is returned to your device.

We do not store the content of these requests or responses on our servers. We use limited, non-identifying operational information (such as timing and error categories) to keep the service running and prevent abuse; this information does not include the content of your messages. Anthropic processes requests under its own terms; Anthropic does not use data submitted through its API to train its models.

Because the text you type into these features is sent for processing, please avoid entering sensitive personal information you would not want processed by a third-party AI service.

What we do not do

  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising software in the app.
  • We do not track your activity across other apps or websites.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
  • We do not require an account or collect your name, email, or contacts to use the app.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, NextRep schedules reminders (such as upcoming study blocks or your daily summary) locally on your device. These are generated on your device from your own schedule and are not sent from or through our servers.

Children's privacy

NextRep is intended for high-school and college-aged student-athletes and older. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because the app does not collect personal information on our servers, it does not build profiles of any users, including younger ones.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
